Privacy Policy — Job Tracker Extension

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you click Autofill or Save, the extension reads the following from the current job posting page: job title, company name, location, salary (if available), job description text, and the page URL. Nothing is collected passively.

How it's used

The data is sent directly to your personal Job Tracker application. It is not shared with any third party.

Storage

Data is stored in your personal Supabase PostgreSQL database and is only accessible to you.
